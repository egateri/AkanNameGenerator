This Program, Akan Name Generator, is designed to help users to know the day of the week they were born and hence identify their Akan Names.

Follow below steps to Know your Akan Name:

  1. Input your Birth Year, Birth Month and Birth Day,
  2. Choose your Gender - Male or Female,
  3. Click the Submit Button to display your Akan Name.
Akan Name Generator:
Enter the Birth Year in YYYY format
Enter the Birth Month in MM format
Enter the Birth Day in DD format

   
   
Choose your Gender as either Male or Female
